## Lessons

- **Vague prompts produce vague results**: "sharpen" is subjective; "eliminate redundancy" and "strengthen verbs" are actionable instructions.
- **Structure over instructions**: A clear process (1, 2, 3) with defined outputs is more effective than paragraph-style instructions.
- **Checklists are powerful**: Adding a verification step at the end ensures quality standards are actually met, not just aimed for.
